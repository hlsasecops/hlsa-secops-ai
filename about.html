Our Story

From red team to AI team

HLSA SecOps started as a cybersecurity firm—running penetration tests, red team operations, and security assessments for businesses that couldn't afford to get hacked. We spent years breaking into systems so our clients could fix them before the bad actors got there first.

Along the way, we noticed something: the same businesses hiring us for security were drowning in manual processes. Their teams were spending 60% of their time on repetitive tasks that could easily be automated. But every automation tool they tried was either insecure by default or required a team of developers to maintain.

So we built the solution ourselves. HLSA SecOps AI is the AI automation division born from our security roots. Every system we build comes with the same rigor we apply to penetration testing—because we know exactly how these systems get exploited when they're built without security in mind.

Today, we help businesses in healthcare, finance, SaaS, and professional services automate their operations with AI—while keeping their data, their clients' data, and their reputation locked down tight.
